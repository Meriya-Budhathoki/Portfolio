My background and professional focus

My academic and professional background is in civil engineering, construction, and infrastructure-related work. Through this course, I explored how artificial intelligence can support the construction and facilities management industry in practical ways.

I am interested in how AI tools can help with project communication, data analysis, scheduling, documentation, design coordination, safety review, and facilities operations. I believe technology can help professionals make better decisions, but human judgment and engineering knowledge remain important.

This website brings together my semester projects, assignments, weekly article summaries, and reflections. Each section shows how my understanding of AI in construction and facilities management developed throughout the course.
