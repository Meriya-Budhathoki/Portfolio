About Me

Hello, I’m Meriya Budhathoki.

I am a graduate student in Construction and Facilities Management with a background in civil engineering. My academic and professional interests include construction technology, transportation, infrastructure, facilities management, estimating, data analysis, and AI-supported decision-making.

Through the AI in Construction and Facilities Management course, I explored how artificial intelligence can help construction professionals improve project communication, automate repetitive tasks, support estimating workflows, analyze project data, and improve safety and planning.

I am especially interested in responsible AI adoption in the architecture, engineering, and construction industry. I believe AI can be a strong productivity tool, but it should support human expertise rather than replace professional judgment.
