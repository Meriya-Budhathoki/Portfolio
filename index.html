Final Project Highlight

My final project, Navigating the New Frontier: AI Adoption in the AEC Industry, explored how engineering professionals use AI in real workflows. The project was based on virtual/email interviews with professionals in bridge/structural engineering and transportation engineering.

The project found that AI is currently being used for documentation, coding support, dashboards, visualization, data analysis, and workflow efficiency, while still requiring human judgment for high-stakes engineering decisions.
